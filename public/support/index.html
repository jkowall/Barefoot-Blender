Purchases and refunds

iOS purchases, cancellations, and refunds are managed through Apple. Android purchases, cancellations, and refunds are managed through Google Play. Use Restore Purchases in the native app after reinstalling or changing devices.

Safety reminder

Barefoot Blender is for trained divers and fill station operators. Always analyze final gas with calibrated analyzers before diving.
