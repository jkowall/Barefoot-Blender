Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

Barefoot Blender is a scuba gas blending and dive planning utility. The app does not use ads, tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or a Barefoot Blender account system.

Data stored on your device

The app stores settings, last-used calculator inputs, custom gas definitions, and blend history locally on your device so the app can work offline. This local data is not sent to Barefoot Blender servers.

Subscription data

Native iOS and Android subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. These providers may process purchase receipts, subscription status, device store identifiers, app user identifiers, and diagnostic data needed to validate purchases and restore access.

Health and dive data

Barefoot Blender does not collect health data, location data, dive profiles, biometric data, or personally identifiable dive records.

Support
